Overview

HOSA – Future Health Professions a global student-led organization that is open to students enrolled in Healthcare Related and Protective Service programs. HOSA’s mission is to empower students to become leaders in the healthcare community, through education, technical skills, collaboration, and community service. In addition to the local BCTC chapter, students will have the opportunity to obtain leadership and competitive event experiences at the State and International Leadership Conferences. Visit www.hosa.org for more information.test
